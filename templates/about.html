About FinBha₹at

FinBha₹at is a web application designed as an alternative to CS50's Finance course project, tailored specifically for the Indian stock market. This tool provides users with a suite of features to manage their virtual stock portfolio effectively:

  • User Authentication: Secure login and registration with hashed passwords and session management.
  • Buy Stocks: Purchase shares of stocks with real-time price updates and manage your portfolio.
  • Sell Stocks: Sell stocks from your portfolio with automatic updates to your cash balance.
  • View Portfolio: Displays current stock holdings, including symbols, quantities, and market values.
  • Transaction History: Detailed history of all stock transactions, including purchases and sales.
  • Get Stock Quotes: Retrieve real-time stock prices and company information from external data services.
  • Stock Lookup: Search for stock symbols and obtain relevant information to make informed trading decisions.
  • Cash Management: Shows your current cash balance and updates it automatically after trades.

Derived from CS50 Problem Set 9 (pset9), FinBha₹at has been customized and extended to meet the needs of the Indian market. The application is now live and ready for use.

To explore the source code or learn more about this project, visit my GitHub repository for additional details.
